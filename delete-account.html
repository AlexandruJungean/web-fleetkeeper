Account & Data Deletion Request

Request the deletion of your FleetKeeper account and all associated personal data.

Important Information

⚠️ Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. Once your account is deleted, all your data will be permanently removed and cannot be recovered.

Before proceeding with account deletion, please ensure you have exported any data you wish to keep. If you are a company administrator, deleting your account will also affect all users and data associated with your company.

Data That Will Be Deleted

The following data associated with your account will be permanently deleted:

Personal information (name, email, phone)
Company data (if you are the owner)
Vehicles and associated information
Drivers and associated information
Documents and expiration terms
Notification history
Settings and preferences
All attached files and images

How to Delete Your Account

Recommended

Option 1: Delete from the App

The fastest and easiest way to delete your account is directly from the FleetKeeper app.

  1. Open FleetKeeper
    Launch the FleetKeeper app on your device
  2. Go to Profile
    Tap on the "More" tab, then select "Profile"
  3. Find Danger Zone
    Scroll down to the "Danger Zone" section at the bottom
  4. Delete Account
    Tap "Delete My Account" button
  5. Confirm Deletion
    Read the warning and confirm by typing your email address

Option 2: Request via Email

If you cannot access the app, you can request account deletion by email.

Email: support@fleetkeeper.app

Subject: Account Deletion Request

Include: The email address associated with your FleetKeeper account

Processing time: Up to 30 days

Data Retention Policy

  • Your data will be deleted within 30 days of your request being processed.
  • Some data may be retained for up to 5 years for legal and tax compliance purposes (e.g., invoices, transaction records).
  • Backup copies are automatically purged within 90 days after account deletion.
  • Anonymized analytics data may be retained for service improvement purposes.
